Data Types:
- int : Stores numbers from -2,147,483,648 to 2,147,483,647
- long: Wider range than integer (-10^12 to 10^12)
- long long: Even bigger range (-10^18 to 10^18)
- float: For decimals
- double: Bigger range of decimals
- string: picks up first word (before space/newline)
    To get entire line, use getline()
    e.g string str;
        getline(cin, str);
    represented with double quotes
- char: To store one of 256 characters in the English dictionary
    represented with single quotes
